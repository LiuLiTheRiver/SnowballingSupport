Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Task Content: Tasks, subtasks, and task descriptions you create
  • Voice Input: Task content input through voice recognition features
  • User Preferences: App settings and personalization options

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, app version
  • Usage Data: App usage frequency, feature usage, error logs
  • Network Information: Network connection status, IP address (for service requests)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide and improve task management functionality
  • Process AI task analysis and breakdown requests
  • Provide voice recognition services
  • Process subscriptions and payments
  • Send service notifications and updates
  • Analyze and improve app performance
  • Provide customer support

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties, except:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To trusted third parties necessary for service provision (such as AI service providers)
  • To comply with legal requirements or protect our rights
  • In the event of business transfer or merger
Third-Party Service Providers:
  • OpenAI: For AI task analysis and breakdown
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management and payment processing
  • Apple: For voice recognition services (SFSpeechRecognizer)

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

Your task data is primarily stored locally on your device using SwiftData. This data is not automatically uploaded to our servers.

4.2 Server Storage

When you use AI features, task content is temporarily sent to our servers for processing. After processing, your task content is not permanently stored on our servers.

4.3 Data Security

  • All network communications are encrypted using HTTPS
  • Access controls and authentication are implemented
  • Regular security audits and updates are conducted
  • Employee access to user data is restricted

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right of Access: View the personal information we collect about you
  • Right of Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal information
  • Right of Erasure: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right of Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Right to Object: Object to the processing of your personal information

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you know that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to servers outside your country or region for processing. We ensure these transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.

8. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. For significant changes, we will notify you through the app or by other means. We recommend reviewing this policy regularly.
